## About QuirkyIT

Founded in Australia, QuirkyIT delivers pragmatic IT and cybersecurity services for small and mid-sized businesses.

We pride ourselves on:

- **Working with you** to understand your business goals.
- **Avoiding one-size-fits-all solutions**; we build bespoke plans.
- **Keeping things simple and maintainable**, while still being secure.

### Why “Quirky”?

Because the best solutions often come from thinking differently. We combine technical know-how with real-world practicality.
