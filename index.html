// 05 — How QuirkyIT Works

Straightforward, scoped, and useful

The process should feel modern and calm: understand the environment, define the right slice of work, deliver something useful, and leave the organisation clearer than before.

01

Discovery and context

Start with the environment, goals, pain points, and constraints. Good advice depends on understanding what actually matters to the organisation.

02

Scope the useful work

Define the service boundary properly, whether that is exposure review, threat briefing, logging uplift, AI workflow support, vulnerability hygiene, or a broader advisory engagement.

03

Deliver practical outcomes

Provide something the business can use, whether that is a clearer picture, a better baseline, a shortlist of recommendations, or a set of next actions tied to the real environment.

04

Support the next move

Follow through into implementation, validation, or follow-up work where it makes sense, so the organisation is not left with a pile of theory and no path forward.
